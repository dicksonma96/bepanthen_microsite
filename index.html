Product Description

Bepanthen SensiWash is developed for daily care of dry, sensitive and eczema-prone skin. It helps maintain the skin’s natural pH and protects it from drying out. It has a calming effect and contributes to a healthy skin. Bepanthen SensiWash contains pro-vitamin B5 and prebiotics that help stabilize the natural skin barrier, supports the skin’s own regeneration and protective microflora. It also contains natural lipids that help protect the skin against dehydration.

How to use Bepanthen SensiWash Daily Gentle Body Wash?

  • Just massage onto wet skin and rinse thoroughly. Bepanthen SensiWash Daily Gentle Body Wash can be used multiple times a day.

Can Bepanthen SensiWash Daily Gentle Body Wash used on babies?

  • Bepanthen SensiWash Daily Gentle Body Wash is considered safe for babies (as of 1 month old) . The product is well tolerated in infants and children.
